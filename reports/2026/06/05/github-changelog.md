## Fix with Copilot for failing Actions now in Pro, Pro+, and Max

- 公開日 (UTC): `2026-06-04 20:46:11`
- リンク: <https://github.blog/changelog/2026-06-04-fix-with-copilot-for-failing-actions-now-in-pro-pro-and-max>

- 公開日 (UTC): `2026-06-04 18:45:04`
